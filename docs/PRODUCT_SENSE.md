# Product Sense

MedSentinel exists to shorten the path from spatial risk to action.

- Every major workflow should reduce time-to-triage for clinicians and safety officers.
- Labels must be plain clinical English.
- Recommendations must be specific and guideline-linked.
- A user should understand what to inspect in the building without parsing internal model jargon.
- Confidence and evidence matter because the system works with partially observed public imagery.
